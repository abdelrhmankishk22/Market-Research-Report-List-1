Lead Zinc Titanates (PZT) are widely used due to their high sensitivity, high piezoelectric constant, and excellent electromechanical coupling. Lead Titanate (PT) provides good mechanical properties and is suitable for high-temperature applications. Lead Magnesium Niobate (PMN) offers high electromechanical coupling, making it ideal for use in ultrasonic transducers. The "Others" category encompasses alternative materials that possess unique properties, such as modified ceramic compositions or composites.
