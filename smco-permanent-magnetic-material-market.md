<p><p>SmCo permanent magnetic material market includes two main types: SmCo5 and Sm2Co17. SmCo5, also known as cobalt samarium, consists of five parts samarium and one part cobalt. It possesses a higher magnetic energy product but lower coercivity compared to Sm2Co17. On the other hand, Sm2Co17, also called cobalt samarium iron, comprises two parts samarium, 13-17 parts cobalt, and some iron. It exhibits higher coercivity and better temperature stability than SmCo5. Both types offer excellent magnetic properties, making them crucial in various applications requiring strong and stable magnetism.</p></p>
