<p><p>Motorcycle Brake By Wire (BBW) Systems refer to the electronic control of braking in motorcycles. Single-channel BBW systems use a single electronic control unit (ECU) to regulate the braking force on both the front and rear wheels. This system is cost-effective and widely used in motorcycles. On the other hand, multiple-channel BBW systems use separate ECUs for the front and rear brakes, allowing for independent control and greater precision in braking force distribution. This system is typically utilized in higher-end motorcycles for enhanced safety and performance.</p></p>
