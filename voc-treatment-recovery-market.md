<p><p>Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs) are harmful pollutants emitted by industrial processes. The VOC Treatment & Recovery market offers various technologies to control and eliminate these pollutants. Regenerative Thermal Oxidation uses high temperatures to convert VOCs into harmless gases. Recuperative Thermal Oxidation also utilizes heat, but in a more energy-efficient manner. Catalytic Oxidation relies on catalysts to accelerate the decomposition of VOCs. Photo-oxidation employs UV light to break down VOCs. Adsorption by Activated Carbon involves capturing VOCs using porous carbon material. Rotor Concentrators concentrate VOCs before treatment. Cryocondensation uses low temperatures to condense and remove VOCs. Other markets may include membrane separation or biofiltration.</p></p>
