<p><p>There are three major types of carbon materials in the market: carbon nanotube (CNT), carbon nanofiber, and nano carbon ball. Carbon nanotubes are cylindrical structures made of carbon atoms, offering excellent mechanical, electrical, and thermal properties. Carbon nanofibers are thin fibers composed primarily of carbon, widely used in various industries due to their high strength and thermal stability. Nano carbon balls are spherical carbon nanoparticles, characterized by their high surface area and unique properties, making them suitable for applications in energy storage, catalysts, and electronic devices.</p></p>
