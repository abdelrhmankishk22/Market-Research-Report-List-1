<p><p>The Agricultural Complementary Photovoltaic Power Station market includes two main types: Crystalline Silicon Photovoltaic Power Station and Thin Film Photovoltaic Power Station. Crystalline Silicon Photovoltaic Power Station utilizes silicon-based solar cells to convert sunlight into electricity. This type offers high efficiency and durability but is relatively expensive. In contrast, Thin Film Photovoltaic Power Station uses thin layers of semiconductors to capture solar energy. It is more cost-effective but has lower efficiency and lifespan. Both types are used in the agricultural sector to generate renewable energy while providing shade for crops.</p></p>
