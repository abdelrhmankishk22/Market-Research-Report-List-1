<p><p>Dust Suppression Control Chemicals market consists of various types of chemicals used to prevent and suppress dust. Hygroscopic salts are substances that absorb moisture from the air, therefore reducing dust particles. Lignosulfonates are derived from wood and have adhesive properties that help bind dust particles together. Petroleum resins are sticky materials that improve dust control. Polymer emulsions are water-based solutions with polymers that form a film on dust particles, preventing them from becoming airborne. Tar and bitumen emulsion products are sticky mixtures that adhere to dust particles, preventing them from dispersing. Other chemicals may include binders, surfactants, or wetting agents.</p></p>
