<p><p>Air source heat pumps are a type of heating system that utilize the outside air to generate heat for indoor spaces. There are two main types of air source heat pumps in the market - air-to-air and air-to-water. Air-to-air heat pumps transfer heat from the outside air to the inside air, providing warmth for residential or commercial spaces. On the other hand, air-to-water heat pumps transfer the heat to a water-based system, such as underfloor heating or radiators, offering efficient heating and hot water solutions for buildings.</p></p>
